Departmental AI Focus: Unique Strengths

Each department is leveraging AI in ways that align with their core functions, showcasing diverse and specialized applications.

💻

CM (Category Management)

CM teams are uniquely utilizing AI for complex data analysis, particularly for Looker calculations, and for generating supplier FAQs, streamlining product information management.

📈

Desk/GPS (Global Partner Support)

Desk/GPS departments are focused on leveraging AI for knowledge retrieval via Glean, assisting new hires, and automating support processes through script generation (e.g., VBA).

👤

CG/CGF (Category Growth & Fulfillment)

CG/CGF teams are using AI to refine professional communication with suppliers and to efficiently code supplier information, enhancing partner relationships and data accuracy.

💼

SAM (Supplier Account Management)

SAM is leveraging AI primarily for translation and initial analysis of survey data, aiming to gain quick insights and improve communication with their supplier base.

📣

ACQ (Acquisition)

ACQ stands out with its innovative use of AI to overcome language barriers, developing custom translation solutions to enhance cross-cultural communication in acquisition efforts.

🎧

HR/Academy

HR/Academy is exploring AI for creative applications like brainstorming and generating rough reference images for vendors, indicating a focus on content creation and ideation.

AI in Action: Success Stories

🔗

Automated Scripting

Employees successfully used AI to generate custom Tamper Monkey and Excel VBA scripts, automating repetitive tasks and saving significant time on manual data processing.

🌐

Cross-Cultural Communication

A custom AI translator was built by compiling a glossary of Wayfair-specific terminology. This tool, powered by Glean and Gemini, overcomes language barriers and improves clarity in global communications.

📊

Advanced Data Insights

AI proved instrumental in formulating complex Looker calculations and queries. This enabled teams to extract deeper insights from business data and improve reporting accuracy.

📄

Email Summarization & Actionable Insights

AI tools are efficiently summarizing lengthy email threads, highlighting key information and suggesting next steps, significantly streamlining communication workflows.

📚

SOP Summarization & Writing Polish

Gemini is being used to quickly summarize complex Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and to polish written communications, ensuring clarity and professionalism.

🔍

Efficient Knowledge Retrieval

Glean has proven highly effective for new hires to quickly find relevant information and documents, accelerating onboarding and access to best practices.
